Rules

The guidelines for a feed to be included.

  • Have own domain name (not just a subdomain)
  • Offer RSS (or atom) feed
  • Not mostly a newsletter
  • Prefer individuals' websites over groups' over companies'
  • Post at least once a month on average
  • Post no more than five time per day, ideally five per week
  • Aim for one hundred feeds, but no more than one hundred and twenty
  • Ignore these guidelines sometimes
